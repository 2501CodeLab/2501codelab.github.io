About Us

Welcome to the Lane Tech CodeLab, an organization of developers consisting of Lane Tech's very own students.

We create websites and applications. Mostly we make things for the Lane community, tools that help our students, staff, and parents.

Even though we have a focus on web technologies, we are open to all languages, anyone can pass by. We want the LTCodeLab to be a place where any developer can come to collaborate with other people, a place where you can gather some feedback on whatever it is you're working on, or just a chill place to work in.
