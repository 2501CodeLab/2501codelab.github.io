LT Student

About

The LT Student App is a portal to resources tailored specifically to Lane Tech students. You can find various sources of info, such as the school calendar, which will appear compact on your phone. You can also check here to see if there are any special bell schedules for today. It even connects to Lane Tech's database to gather information like the amount of demerits you have, or even see your grades from previous grade levels.

Lost & Found

About

The LT Lost & Found app is a community-driven app that helps students find and report items found around Lane Tech. Take a picture and create a post on the thing you've found, and people can visit the site to check if their lost item has been found by someone.
